Our Commitment to Safeguarding

Askeaton and Ballysteen Parish is committed to providing safe environments for all children and vulnerable adults. The parish fully implements the national standards of the National Board for Safeguarding Children in the Catholic Church in Ireland (NBSCCCI) and the Towards Healing and Safeguarding Trust frameworks.

If a child or vulnerable adult is in immediate danger

Parish Code of Conduct

All volunteers and persons working with children or vulnerable adults in our parish must:

  • Hold a current Garda Vetting clearance through the Diocese of Limerick
  • Have completed NBSCCCI Safe Safeguarding training
  • Adhere to the Diocesan Code of Conduct for Church Personnel
  • Never be alone with a child or vulnerable adult in an unsupervised setting
  • Report any safeguarding concerns immediately to the DLP
